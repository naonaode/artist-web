# 行动准则 / Action Guidelines

1. **小规模实验优先 (Small-scale Experiments First)**: 
   - 做任何大的项目的执行之前，请先小规模的实验在行动。
   - Before executing any large projects or mass operations (like bulk downloading, global refactoring, etc.), always conduct a small-scoped, isolated experiment to validate the approach before scaling up to full production.
